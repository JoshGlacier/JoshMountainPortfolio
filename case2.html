Vancouver Whitecaps FC

I've had the priviledge to have been a Sponsorship Photographer for the Vancouver Whitecaps since January 2019.


​

My position involved following a dedicated shotlist, taking these photos, and distributing them to Stakeholders.

Stakeholders included the 'Caps Crew Partnership Coordinator; Claire Villet. Reporting on the status of photos taken, content acquired, and following shotlist. This project included work that would be distributed to major corporations such as: EA, Capital Direct, Hotels.com, Husquvarna, etc, The Primary Stakeholder was Bell, as they are the primary sponsor of the Whitecaps.
